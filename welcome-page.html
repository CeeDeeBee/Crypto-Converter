Welcome To Crypto Toolbox!

To get started click the icon in the upper right of your screen or browse this page to see how Crypto Toolbox works. You can click the ? in the lower right of the Options Page to return here at any time.

Home Page

The Home Page is the first page you see whenever you open Crypto Toolbox. This page displays the latest price and twenty four hour percent change of your chosen currencies. The color of the circle around the currency indicates whether the price has gone up or down in the last twenty four hours. Clicking on the name and symbol of any currency will take you to the cryptocompare.com webpage for said currency. Currencies listed on the Home Page are fully customizible and can be added or removed from the Options Page.

News Page

The News Page is a currated list of top articles in crypto from the last twenty four hours. Articles can be viewed by clicking the blue headline. The News Page can be accessed by clicking the News tab along the top of the Crypto Toolbox screen.

Portfolio Page

The Portfolio Page is where you can view the status of your crypto holdings. Once you have entered holdings from the Options Page you will see a pie chart and table displaying statistcs about the performance of your portfolio. Clicking on any of the colored boxes displayed to the right of the pie chart will toggle said currency in the pie chart and table. In the table the 24 HR column displays the ammount of fiat currency gained or lost in the last twenty four hours. The Gain/Loss column displays the ammount of fiat currency gained or lost since purchasing your holdings. The Portfolio Page can be accessed by clicking the Portfolio tab along the top of the Crypto Toolbox screen.

Convert Page

On the Convert Page you can convert between any one of the supported crypto currencies and the twenty eight supported fiat currencies. To choose which currencies to convert between simply click the drop down menu listing the currencies and type the name of the currency you wish to select. Below the manual conversion section a few static conversions are listed for the first four Home Page currencies you have selected. The Convert Page can be accessed by clicking the Convert tab along the top of the Crypto Toolbox screen.

Options Page

The Options Page is where you can edit the settings for Crypto Toolbox. The Options Page can be accessed by clicking the Options tab along the top of the Crypto Toolbox sccreen.

Home Page Settings

To add or remove a currency from the Home Page search for it in the searchbox then click the add or remove button in the Home Page column listed in the table in the center of the page.

Price Alerts

To add or edit price alerts search for the desired currency then click the add or edit button under the Alerts column listed in the table in the center of the page. Once the popup has loaded you can enter Above and/or Below prices for which you would like to receive alerts.

Portfolio Page Settings

To add or edit holdings in your Crypto Toolbox portfolio search for the currency you wish to edit then click on the add or edit button under the Portfolio column listed in the table in the center of the page. Once the popup has loaded you can enter the ammount of crypto currency you own along with how much you paid for that share.

Base Currency

Under the Base Currency header you can select any of the six supported base currencies. This is the fiat currency that determines the prices listed on the Home and Portfolio Pages. It is important to note that each base currency has some unsupported crypto currencies.

Alert Frequency

Under the Alert Frequency header you can enter how frequently you would like to get price alerts. It is important to note that on MacOS you must clear previous alerts in order to receive new ones.

Contact

To contact about Crypto Toolbox you can email afterbangsoftware@gmail.com, leave a review on the chrome webstore page or reach out to me (the developer) on Twitter @colin_barnes. Any feedback is greatly appreciated!
